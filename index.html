About

Amadeus is a doctor, polymath, inventor, Olympian, adventurer, designer, musician, magician, and father. He has served in the National Defense Association and is a champion chess player. With such an impressive record it should come as no surprise that he is also a world-renowned sculptor and jazz enthusiast!

In his spare time, Amadeus enjoys time with the family and consumes a rather alarming quantity of authentic Indian chai tea.
